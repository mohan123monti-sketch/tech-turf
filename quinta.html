About Quinta

Quinta is the official aerospace division of Tech Turf, dedicated to advancing experimental rocketry, atmospheric research, and next-generation flight systems.

Our work blends engineering discipline with bold innovation, pushing young talent toward real aerospace breakthroughs.

From small-scale rocket prototypes to sensor-rich flight computers, Quinta develops accessible, research-driven solutions that bring aerospace technology within reach for students, researchers, and future innovators.

Our Mission

To design safe, reliable, and data-driven aerospace systems that support scientific research, hands-on learning, and long-term space innovation.

Our Vision

To grow into a globally recognized aerospace hub creating advanced rockets, reusable flight platforms, AI-assisted avionics, and scalable mission technologies.

What We Build

Pushing the boundaries of young Indian aerospace innovation.

1. Research Rockets

Quinta develops rockets that test propulsion, structural stability, sensor integration, and flight performance.

2. Flight Computers

Microcontroller-powered avionics with IMU (MPU6050), Barometric (BMP280), Gas sensors, and 32GB logging.

3. Solid Propulsion

Continuous R&D to improve thrust profiling, grain shaping, chamber safety, nozzle geometry, and performance.

4. Recovery & Safety

Parachute deployment concepts, aerodynamic fins, impact protection, wireless ignition, and safe-zone protocols.

5. Atmospheric Data

Environmental monitoring modules for pressure, temperature, humidity, and air-quality analysis.

6. Wireless Control

Secure long-distance ignition and planned future communication modules for real-time telemetry.
